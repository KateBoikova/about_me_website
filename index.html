Why web development?
Since childhood I was fascinated with computers and the thing you can do using them. In 2020 after the pandemic hit the world I realised how much our modern life depends on technology. And I came to realisation that I want to be a part of it. I want to build things that are user-friendly, that help people in their everyday like and overall a pleasure to use. I love the fact that technology is a place where you learn something new every day. I enjoy problem solving and getting to know how things work "under the hood". I am thrilled at the opportunity to work with like-minded people, learn from the experience of seasoned specialists, share my view and be a part of the team.
